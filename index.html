About me

Hi, I'm Gal, a starting web developer who has recently completed this assignment for HackerU Academy, which involved writing this portfolio site and 6 landing pages using HTML and CSS.

I enjoy writing code a lot and it does get quite tough at times, especially when I get stuck on the small details, but I'm learning to accept that perfection is not always attainable.

While I currently know HTML, CSS and I have prior experience with Python and PowerShell scripting, I'm interested in learning more about JavaScript, C# and maybe even C++ to expand my knowledge and skills as a developer. My long-term goal is to use my skills in development to make a positive impact on the world. I believe that this field provides limitless options to explore and connect with various industries.

Outside of coding, I enjoy playing video games, cooking and playing musical instruments. I currently work in IT as a support technician, where I got a deep understanding of windows enterprise environment and servers. This knowledge will be a big bonus for me as a starting developer, and I hope to continue building upon it in the future.
